A little bit about myself.

My name is Frances MacKercher and I am passionate about improving health equity through technology and design.

Born and raised in Ann Arbor, MI, I moved to Southern California to study Neuroscience as an undergraduate at Claremont McKenna College. My interest in healthcare along with a passion for creative processes and behavioral neuroscience led me to pursue a Masters degree in Health Informatics at the University of Michigan.

My education and professional experience has allowed me to develop a diverse skillset, with an emphasis on front-end development and UX design.
